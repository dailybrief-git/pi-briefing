Thailand's visa-free stay poised to shrink from 60 to 30 days for dozens of nationalities

Cabinet approved (19 May 2026) cutting the standard visa-exemption from 60 to 30 days for most eligible nationalities, including Canada, Poland and Romania — not yet in force, pending Royal Gazette publication. Other travel-advisory sources give conflicting current-rule descriptions (some say 60 days still stands, one implies US travellers already face a tighter 30-day reality). Net effect: entry rules are in flux and worth tracking before they hit booking behaviour.
