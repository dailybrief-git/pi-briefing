Fintech regulationRelevance: HighConfirmed - FCA effective date

UK Buy Now Pay Later comes under FCA regulation from 15 July

BNPL lenders must now meet FCA authorisation, affordability-check and complaints-handling rules. Industry reaction frames it as overdue 'plumbing' change rather than a shock, and notes it narrows the regulatory gap with the EU's revised Consumer Credit Directive, which brings BNPL into consumer credit rules there too.

What

Why
