Thai visa policyRelevance: HighPrimary - travel/visa trade press

Thailand slashes visa-free stays to 30 days for 45+ nationalities, tightens border screening

Visa-free stays cut from up to 60-90 days down to 30 days for many nationalities including Poland, Canada and Romania, with mandatory Thailand Digital Arrival Card (TDAC) screening and stricter overstay penalties (fines plus re-entry bans up to 10 years). Directly affects the long-stay leisure and digital-nomad travellers who anchor Chiang Mai's low season.

▶ Why it matters / what to watch
What
30-day visa-free cap now applies broadly; TDAC mandatory for all entrants regardless of visa status
Why it matters
Shorter permitted stays could shrink average length-of-stay and repeat-visit patterns for long-stay guests in Chiang Mai's rental and hospitality market
