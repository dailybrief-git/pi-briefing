Cabinet finalises visa overhaul: 30-day cap for 59 countries, VOA/15-day for others

The Thai Cabinet sanctioned the visa reform on 14 July, scrapping the 60-day visa-free stay introduced in 2024. 59 countries including all EU states and India now get 30 days visa-free; other nationalities receive 15-day exemption or Visa-on-Arrival. This confirms and finalises the cut flagged earlier this month.
