Fintech regulationRelevance: HighPrimary - trade press

FCA's BNPL rules come into force today

Buy-now-pay-later lending falls under FCA regulation from 15 July, bringing affordability checks and disclosure standards into UK consumer credit law and narrowing the gap with the EU's revised Consumer Credit Directive. Industry reaction has focused on the payments 'plumbing' underneath BNPL checkouts as much as on consumer protection.

▶ Why it matters / what to watch
What
BNPL formally brought under FCA consumer credit regulation as of today, 15 July 2026
Why it matters
Narrows the UK-EU regulatory gap on point-of-sale lending; directly affects any checkout or SaaS product embedding BNPL
