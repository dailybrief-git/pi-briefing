FCA softens crypto capital charge as final rules bed in

Fresh legal analysis of the FCA's finalised cryptoasset regime (briefed yesterday) reveals the operational-risk capital requirement for stablecoin issuers (K-SII) has been cut from 2% to 1%, with the safeguarding K-factor renamed K-RCS to cover a wider set of assets. A targeted softening versus the original consultation.

▶ Why it matters / what to watch
What
Stablecoin issuer capital charge halved to 1%; safeguarding factor broadened
Why
Lowers compliance cost for UK stablecoin issuers just as the regime goes live, relevant to any fintech in that space
